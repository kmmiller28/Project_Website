Troy's Kitchen + Wine Bar was born through a love of superior meals, fine wine and fellowship with family & friends. The desire to provide exceptionally upscale experiences to the Troy area has been a constant and continuing goal. Our vision for Troy's Kitchen + Wine Bar is to present Troy with an experience that inspires a feeling of pride in our community and our regional heritage. We've tried our best to create a freshly updated, fun & lively atmosphere that is laid-back, but doesn't sacrifice the sophistication that makes getting out of the house worth it! We've created a unique menu that features a modern take on simple, yet delicious southern & gulf-coastal inspired recipes and paired it with a top notch wine menu. We are so happy to call Troy home and we want to give you guys a place that you're proud to represent...a place you want to bring out-of-town family and friends. After all, it's all about celebrating and getting social!
